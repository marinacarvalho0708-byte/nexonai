Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

At Neuxon AI, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform at neuxonai.com (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication data (if using Google or Apple sign-in).
  • Conversation data: Messages you send to Neuxon AI are processed to generate responses. If you are logged in, conversation history is stored to provide continuity across sessions.
  • Usage data: We collect anonymous usage statistics such as message count per day to enforce free plan limits.
  • Payment information: If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card or payment details.
  • Technical data: Basic technical information such as browser type and device type may be collected for security and performance purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Process your conversations and generate personalized responses
  • Save your conversation history (for logged-in users)
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Enforce usage limits for free plan users
  • Communicate with you about the Service, including important updates
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Conversation Data and AI Processing

Messages you send to Neuxon AI are processed by our AI system to generate responses. This processing is powered by the Anthropic Claude API. Conversations may be used in aggregate, anonymized form to improve the quality of the Service.

We do not sell your conversation content to third parties or use it for targeted advertising.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your account data and conversation history (for logged-in users) are stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database service. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Local conversation history for users without an account is stored only in your browser's local storage and is not transmitted to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the Service:

  • Anthropic: AI model provider that processes your messages to generate responses. Subject to Anthropic's privacy policy.
  • Supabase: Database and authentication service for storing account data and conversation history.
  • Stripe: Payment processor for subscription billing. Subject to Stripe's privacy policy.
  • Vercel: Hosting provider for the Service.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data and conversation history for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and conversation history will be deleted within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@neuxonai.com.

8. Cookies

The Service uses minimal cookies necessary for authentication and session management. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed by our third-party service providers in countries other than your own. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

privacy@neuxonai.com

Your privacy matters to us. We are committed to being transparent about how we handle your data and to protecting your personal information.
